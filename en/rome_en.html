Rome - The Eternal City

The Eternal City

Rome, a city that encapsulates the historical layers of many cultures, invites you to step into the enchanting world of the Roman Empire, enjoy Italian cuisine, and explore its ancient forums and magnificent structures.

Top Attractions in Rome

  • Colosseum: The largest and most ancient amphitheater in the world.
  • Roman Forum: The center of political and social life in ancient Rome.
  • Trevi Fountain: The most famous fountain where you throw a coin and make a wish.
